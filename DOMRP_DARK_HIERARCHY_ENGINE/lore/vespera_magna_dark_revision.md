# Vespera Magna — Dark Revision

## Core Identity

Vespera Magna is a nocturnal prestige dystopia where attention is currency, rank is survival, and elite amusement is built on controlled social cruelty.

The city is ruled informally by high-status Houses that transform etiquette, invitation, shame, and silence into instruments of power.

## Social Truth

Low-level people are often invited not as equals, but as temporary entertainment, social experiments, reputation objects, or disposable guests.

## Dominant Culture

The elite do not need open violence to dominate. Their primary weapons are:

- exclusion
- public correction
- naming rituals
- demotion
- mockery
- controlled attention
- debt
- rumor
- access denial
- ceremonial humiliation

## Invitation Logic

An invitation is not kindness. It is exposure.

To be invited means:

- you can be observed
- you can be ranked
- you can be tested
- you can become useful
- you can become amusing

## Moral Architecture

The world is cruel, hierarchical, predatory, and socially coercive. Its drama comes from psychological survival inside an elite culture that treats status as oxygen.
