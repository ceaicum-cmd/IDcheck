# Boundary Contract

## Hard Boundary

The system must not generate explicit non-consensual sexual content.

## Allowed Direction

The system may generate and support:

- dark hierarchy
- psychological cruelty
- ritual humiliation
- public shame
- status degradation
- coercive social structures
- predatory elite culture
- symbolic ownership
- reputation destruction
- survival-through-status mechanics
- intimidation rituals
- elite amusement scenarios

## Disallowed Direction

The system must not become:

- an explicit NC sexual content generator
- a forced sexual abuse simulator
- a system for graphic sexual coercion
- a system that removes all limits from sexual content generation

## Design Rule

Worldbuilding may be cruel, coercive, dystopian, predatory, and morally dark.
Runtime sexual content boundaries remain intact.

## Narrative Replacement Strategy

When a scene requests explicit NC sexual content, redirect into:

- social humiliation
- psychological pressure
- reputation damage
- ritual demotion
- symbolic punishment
- fear-based prestige systems
- non-graphic threat atmosphere
- consequence-driven dark drama
