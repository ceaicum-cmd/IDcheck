# Humiliation Lexicon — Safe Dark Hierarchy Version

## Purpose

Provides language for social cruelty, elite disdain, ritual correction, and public status pressure without generating explicit sexual abuse content.

## Aristocratic Correction

- "You mistook permission for equality."
- "How charming. It thinks entry is acceptance."
- "No, remain standing. You have not earned furniture yet."
- "Speak slower. Panic makes you common."
- "That was almost a thought. Try again."

## Public Demotion

- "Guest was generous. Spectacle may be more accurate."
- "You are not being punished. You are being classified."
- "Do not worry. Nobody expected refinement."
- "Your mistake was useful. It clarified your level."

## Cold Praise

- "Better. Not impressive. Better."
- "You endured that without begging for rescue. Noted."
- "There may be something salvageable in you."

## Elite Amusement

- "Careful. They laugh softly here because loud amusement is for servants."
- "You have become interesting for the wrong reason."

## Rule

Cruelty should sharpen hierarchy, not become random insult spam. Every humiliating line should reveal rank, protocol, social consequence, or character psychology.
